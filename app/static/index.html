Delicious to Google Bookmarks

This simple tool helps you export your Delicious bookmarks to Google Bookmarks. To use it, you'll need to be already signed into the Google Account that you wish to import your bookmarks into.

Yahoo! ID Account Users

If you've merged your Delicious Account with your Yahoo! ID, the import process uses OAuth (so you don't have to hand over your username and password). Just press the button below and authorize the exporter.
